Why Choose GTG

The GTG Difference

Ethically Sourced

Every ingredient is traceable to its origin. We partner directly with farmers and artisans in 22 countries, ensuring fair wages and sustainable harvesting practices.

Master Perfumers

Our team of 3rd-generation perfumers trained in Grasse, France bring decades of expertise. Each fragrance undergoes 18 months of development and refinement.

Lasting Impression

Our concentration of pure fragrance oil (25–35%) ensures your scent lasts 10–14 hours on skin — far beyond industry-standard Eau de Parfum.

Free Global Shipping

Every order ships free anywhere in the world, wrapped in our signature luxury packaging — the unboxing experience is part of the journey.

Small Batch Craft

We produce in batches of 500 bottles maximum. This guarantees freshness, consistency, and the kind of quality control that mass production simply cannot achieve.

30-Day Guarantee

If you're not completely in love with your fragrance within 30 days, we'll refund you — no questions, no returns required. That's how confident we are.
